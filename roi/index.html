tips_and_updates What affects your results

  • Flyer design — a clean, eye-catching layout gets noticed and kept.
  • Print quality — good paper signals a serious, trustworthy business.
  • The offer — a clear deal or discount with a deadline drives responses.
  • Target area — delivering near your business lifts results a lot.
  • Business type — food, retail and local services convert best.
  • Timing & frequency — repeated drops build recognition and trust.
