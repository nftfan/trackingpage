help_outline How It Works – Step by Step

  1. map 1. Draw Your Target Area: Use the interactive map to draw a rectangular area where you want your flyers distributed.
    • Click the "Draw" tool on the map interface.
    • Select and drag to create a rectangle that defines your delivery zone (up to 30km²).
    • You can zoom and adjust the rectangle to fine-tune the area.
    • This zone defines where your flyers will be physically delivered door-to-door.
  2. analytics 2. Estimate Flyers & Campaign Cost: Click the “Analyze Area” button after drawing your zone.
    • The system will automatically calculate:
      • ✔ Estimated number of households/addresses
      • ✔ Required number of flyers
      • ✔ Required LFC credits (1 LFC = 1 flyer)
      • ✔ Total campaign cost in EUR
    • These calculations help you understand the scope and budget needed for your campaign.
  3. upload_file 3. Upload Your Flyer(s): Submit the flyer you want distributed in the selected area.
    • You can upload:
      • 🖼 High-quality images (PNG, JPG)
      • 📄 Print-ready PDF files
    • Multiple designs? Upload more than one and specify how they should be used.
    • Once uploaded, preview your flyer to ensure quality and correctness.
  4. key 4. Enter Your LFC Wallet Key: Paste your wallet address (LFC credit key) to proceed with the booking.
    • The system checks your balance in real-time.
    • If you don’t have enough LFC, a message will prompt you to purchase more before submitting.
    • Your wallet remains secure – we only check credit availability.
  5. email 5. Track Campaign Progress: Enter your campaign ID to see the campaign status and results.
    • You will receive a unique campaign ID after starting your campaign.
    • Bookmark the link provided to get live progress updates (link is live within 24h).
