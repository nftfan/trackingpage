Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a worker?

expand_more

After posting a job and confirming via email, we can typically find workers for jobs posted at least 24 hours in advance. For urgent same-day needs, we do our best to accommodate but cannot guarantee availability.

How does the payment process work?

expand_more

After job completion, you'll receive an invoice through the TENTOO freelance platform. Payment terms are 14 days from invoice receipt. We handle all tax and social security requirements so you can focus on your business.

Can I request the same worker again?

expand_more

Yes! If you've had a positive experience with a particular worker, you can request them for future jobs. Subject to their availability, we're happy to arrange this.

What if I need to cancel a job?

expand_more

Please contact us at info@leuvenflyers.com as soon as possible. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the job start time incur no fee. Last-minute cancellations may incur a nominal fee to compensate the worker.

How are workers vetted?

expand_more

All workers on our platform go through a screening process including identity verification, reference checks, and a brief interview. We ensure they have the necessary skills and reliability for temporary work.
