Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?
Yes. Listing your business on FlyerZip is completely free — no fees and no card required. We're inviting local businesses to join while the platform grows.
I'm not a designer. Can I still make a good flyer?
Absolutely. After you fill in the form, you'll receive a ready-to-use ChatGPT prompt. Just copy it, paste it into ChatGPT, add your logo, and it creates a professional flyer for you in minutes. No design experience needed.
How long does the whole thing take?
About 10 minutes from start to finish: fill in your details, generate and download your flyer with the AI prompt, then upload it with your location and keywords.
How do customers find my flyer?
Visitors search FlyerZip by business name, category, location, or service. That's why adding plenty of relevant services and keywords matters — each one is another way for customers to discover you.
Can I list more than one flyer?
For now we allow one flyer per business so listings stay clean and fair. Additional flyers and discount cards are coming soon.
