Flyer Print Guidelines

Print Requirements

  • Your flyers must be of A6 size.
  • It should be 250gsm.
  • It could be mate or glossy.
  • It should be printed on both sides: Front and Back.
  • flyer.be is a good website for printing, they do home delivery.
  • Once your flyers are at your place, inform us to pick up the flyers for distribution.
  • Ask for help: leuvenfd@mail.com before order printing.
