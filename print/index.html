Print Requirements

Essential

  • Your flyers must be of A6 size (105mm × 148mm).

    A6 is approximately 1/4 the size of a standard A4 sheet, making it perfect for hand-to-hand distribution.

  • Paper weight must be 250gsm for optimal durability and quality feel.

    250gsm provides a sturdy feel without being too rigid, making it easy for customers to handle.

  • Finish can be either matte or glossy, depending on your design preference.

    Matte works better for elegant, subdued designs, while glossy makes colors pop and provides more durability.

  • Flyers must be printed on both sides: Front and Back.

    Double-sided printing maximizes your advertising space and ensures customers have all necessary information.

  • Once your flyers are delivered to your location, inform us to arrange pickup for distribution.

Design Specifications

Technical

  • Set up your design with 3mm bleed on all sides.

    Bleed ensures that your design extends to the edge of the paper after cutting.

  • Keep important content at least 5mm away from edges.

    This safety margin prevents important information from being cut off during trimming.

  • Use high-resolution images (minimum 300 DPI).

    Lower resolution images will appear pixelated or blurry when printed.

  • Use CMYK color mode for all design elements.

    RGB colors may appear different when printed as they're converted to CMYK during the printing process.

Pre-Print Checklist

  • Verify all text is spelled correctly and free of grammatical errors
  • Confirm contact information and QR codes are accurate and functional
  • Check that all images are high resolution (300 DPI minimum)
  • Ensure promo code is clearly visible and matches your tracking system
  • Review final PDF in "Print Preview" mode before submitting
