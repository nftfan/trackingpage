LeuvenFlyers - Quick Guide

Offline Marketing Solutions with Tracking Features

LeuvenFlyers helps you send your message to thousands of people and track their conversions in real time.

Our Process

  1. Design an eye-catching flyer that includes a QR code and promo code.
  2. We create a promo page where customers can enter the code to download a gift voucher.
  3. Upload the URL to the QR code on your flyer, print high-quality flyers (A6 size, 250gsm, glossy).
  4. Bring flyers to us, or we’ll pick them up for distribution.

Our Distribution System

We divide the distribution area into zones. Each zone requires 1,000 flyers. On our website, zones are color-coded:

  • Red: Flyers not distributed yet.
  • Blue: Flyers delivered to all letterboxes.
