The Old Way vs. The Smart Way

For decades, offline marketing was a one-way broadcast. You printed 5,000 flyers, distributed them across the city, and then… waited. Maybe you saw a small bump in foot traffic. Maybe you didn't. You had no way of knowing how many people read your flyer, visited your website because of it, or became a customer. It was marketing in the dark.

But in 2026, that excuse no longer exists. The combination of a well-designed flyer, a QR code, a personalised promo code, and a dedicated landing page creates something entirely new: offline marketing you can actually measure. Every flyer becomes a tracked touchpoint. Every scan is a data point. Every redeemed code is a proven conversion.

The core insight: Flyers create trust and attention in the physical world. Digital tools — QR codes, landing pages, promo codes — capture that attention and convert it into measurable results. Together, they're unstoppable.

Promo Codes: Your Offline-to-Online Attribution Tool

A promo code is deceptively simple. But for a business owner, it's the most powerful attribution tool that exists in offline marketing. When a customer types LEUVEN20 at checkout, you know with 100% certainty they came from your flyer campaign.

This closes the attribution loop completely. No more guessing. No more "I think some of this revenue is from the flyers." You know exactly what your campaign delivered.

Smart tip: Use zone-specific codes (LEUVEN-NORTH, LEUVEN-SOUTH) to track which neighbourhoods convert best. Use time-limited codes (expire in 7 days) to create urgency and get people to act fast.

Flyer with promo code → customer redeems online → you see exactly what the campaign generated

5

The Landing Page: Where Flyers Become Customers

This is the single most overlooked piece of the puzzle. Businesses spend money on flyers, print a QR code on them — and then point it to their homepage. That's like catching a fish and then letting it go.

A dedicated landing page is built for one purpose: convert the person who just scanned your flyer. No navigation menu. No blog links. No distractions. Just the offer, the proof, and the button.

Anatomy of a high-converting flyer landing page — every element has one job

⚡

The golden rule: Your landing page should have exactly one call to action. Not a menu. Not links to your blog. One button. One goal. Studies show that removing navigation from landing pages increases conversions by up to 100%.

Your 5-Step Action Plan to Launch This Week

You don't need a big marketing budget or a tech team to run this system. Here's everything you need to get started in under a week:

01
Design Your Flyer
Use AI tools or brief our team. Make the offer crystal clear. One headline, one offer, one QR code. Resist adding too much text.
02
Create Your Landing Page
Free tools like Carrd, Mailchimp, or even a simple Wix page work fine. One goal: get the visitor to claim the offer.
03
Generate a Tracked QR Code
Use QR.io or Bitly QR codes — they give you scan counts, location data, and time-of-day analytics for free.
04
Set Up a Promo Code
Create a unique code in your POS or e-commerce system. Set an expiry date 7–10 days out to drive urgency and get people acting fast.
05
Book Your Distribution
Tell LeuvenFlyers which zones you want, how many flyers, and your campaign dates. We handle the rest — with GPS proof of every delivery.
