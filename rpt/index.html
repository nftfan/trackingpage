About the Restaurant

Welcome to Lahori Bites. Experience the authentic flavors of Punjab right in the heart of Karachi. From home-style BBQ and hand-rolled chapatis to luscious lassi and vibrant curries, we bring you classic Lahori taste in a relaxed, family-friendly environment.

What to eat here?

Chicken Karahi

A fiery and flavor-packed chicken curry cooked in a traditional wok with fresh tomatoes, ginger, green chilies, and our secret blend of Lahori spices.

Beef Seekh Kebab

Juicy, melt-in-your-mouth minced beef skewers seasoned with aromatic herbs and freshly grilled to perfection over hot charcoal.
