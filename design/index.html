Flyer Design Guidelines

Design Requirements

  • Your flyers must feature an attractive offer to engage your customers.
  • Include a QR Code on your flyer, which should link to your promo page.
  • Display a clear promo code on the flyer that customers can use when visiting your promo page.
  • You have the option to use different designs for the front and back sides of the flyer, or the same design for both sides.
  • Canva.com is recommended for designing your flyer. Make sure to select the A4 size format on Canva.
