Simple, Transparent Payment

  • We utilize an official payroll service in Belgium to handle all administration and hiring of flyer distributor for your campaign, these distributors could by students trained for flyer distribution by LeuvenFlyers.
  • After your flyers are fully distributed, you’ll receive an email to approve the distributor’s work.
  • Once approved, our payroll partner will invoice you for the agreed campaign amount—no hidden fees, no payment surprises.
  • When you pay the invoice directly to the payroll service, the payment will be completed for us.
  • LeuvenFlyers will not send you any invoice.
