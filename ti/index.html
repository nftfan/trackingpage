🚀 LeuvenFlyers Training

🔐 Login & Setup

1️⃣ Go to: leuvenflyers.com/ms400
2️⃣ Enter your FF ID (ask in staff chat 💬)
3️⃣ Enter your SEG number (ask in chat 💬)
4️⃣ Click Focus
5️⃣ Click Start ▶️ and enter your first name

📍 Starting Point

➡️ Go to the red box on the map
💬 When you arrive, message: "I am at starting point"

🗺️ How to Deliver

➡️ Follow the purple line on the map
📬 When you see a mailbox:
YES Click Green
📄 Put flyer inside
ORANGE Click Orange button

🏢 Multiple Mailboxes

➡️ For buildings with many mailboxes:
🔁 Repeat for each mailbox:
YES → deliver → ORANGE

🚫 Red Sticker Rule

📬 Mailbox with red sticker:
NO Click Red
❌ Do NOT deliver flyer
❌ Do NOT click Orange

🏁 Finish

➡️ When you reach end of segment:
💬 Inform in chat: "Segment completed"

⚠️ Important Rules

✔️ Always follow Green → Deliver → Orange
✔️ Follow purple line strictly
✔️ Report start & finish in chat
